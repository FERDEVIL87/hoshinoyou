Hoshi no You Ni (2009)
"Hoshi no Yō ni..." (星ほしのように…, lit. "Like the Stars...") is a song recorded by Japanese singer Misia, from her ninth studio album, Just Ballade. It was released simultaneously with the album on December 16, 2009, through Ariola Japan. "Hoshi no Yō ni..." is the theme song to the kaiju film Mega Monster Battle: Ultra Galaxy.

Artist: Misia
Released: 2009
Album: Just Ballade
